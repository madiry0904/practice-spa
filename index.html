You Are Not Alone

Mental health is one of the most important parts of your overall health as research demonstrates it's influence on your physical health. Millions of people around the world struggle with moderate to severe mental health every day. Mental heath doesn't discriminate and affects people's ability to work, perform, create, compete, laugh, love, and participate in their everyday life. The CDC estimates that approximately 50% of all Americans are diagnosed with a mental illness or disorder at some point in their lives. In the US alone, 1 in 5 adults experience any mental illness while 1 in 25 experience a serious mental illness. Because of this widespread prevalence it is essential for us to establish healthy mental health habits to reach out optimal performance levels.

Anxiety disorders are the most common form of mental illness in the U.S.,
affecting 40 million adults aged 18+. Anxiety disorders are highly treatable,
yet only 36.9% of those suffering receive adequate treatment.

Because of this widespread prevalence, it is imperative we establish healthy mental health habits to ensure we live our greatest lives. This is why we created our quiz! Here at Mental Health Made Simple we believe in the power of easy, digestable exercises to get your mental health to it's optimal level!
